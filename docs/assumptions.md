# Assumptions

## Users & Scale

- **User base:** Small team (< 10 people) initially.
- **Geographic:** Single region. No multi-region requirements yet.

---

## Data & CSV Uploads

- **Required columns:** store_id, sku, product_name, price, date.
- **Data quality:** Users responsible for data validation before upload.
- **Time zones:** All dates in same timezone. No timezone conversion.

---

No Role-based access (admin vs viewer)
